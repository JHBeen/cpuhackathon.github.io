Camp Mobile has its roots from Korea's dominant web portal, NAVER Corporation.

CAMP27, a forerunner of Camp Mobile, was established for independent projects on the 27th floor of NAVER's Green Factory.
From there, global No.1 social App 'BAND' came into the world.

Ever since it was spun off as a subsidiary of NAVER in 2013,
Camp Mobile has been building global-scale mobile services with its accumulated expertise and technology.
